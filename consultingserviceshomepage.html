Crowleys Consultancy

We are a consultancy firm based in Cork City. We have been operating since 2012 and at present we have helped over 1000 customers with their business'.
Our business is ethical and family run and we are passionate about all aspects of the services we offer.
We employee over 100 members of staff and we offer competitive salaries.
We open every week day at 9am and close for the day at 5pm.
Should you have any queries please do not hesitate to contact us and we will try our best to help you.
Our work is always intended to provide a clear benefit to the organizations we work with in both the short and long term.
We focus our resources, leverage our global scale, and drive excellence in all we do while enhancing our partner-like culture to ensure we are collaborative, authentic, and forward-thinking. We have a distinctive, collegial culture that transcends organizational and geographic boundaries.
Our consultants are down to earth, approachable, and have a passion for doing innovative client work.
We always seek to deliver both immediate impact and growing advantage to our clients and our people.
Our culture is premised on liking what you do and whom you do it with, minimum bureaucracy and low overhead.
We value leadership and creating value/making a difference with our clients.
